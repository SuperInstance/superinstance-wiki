**plato-nervous** is the core Rust crate of the PLATO Nervous System, implementing a 5-layer signal chain that processes sensor readings through algorithmic deadband filters (Layer 0), a 350M nano model (Layer 1), room-specific LoRA adapters (Layer 2), a 1.2B fleet coordinator (Layer 3), and cloud LLM fallback (Layer 4), with each layer resolving what it can so that <1% of situations ever reach the cloud. The system emphasizes local autonomy by using deadband filtering to eliminate false positives before they reach neural models, and tracks distillation quality via a conservation ratio across layer transitions. It sits at the center of a broader ecosystem—including plato-vision-jepa, plato-audio-jepa, plato-browser, and luciddreamer-ai—that together provide room-specific intelligence for the PLATO fleet architecture.
