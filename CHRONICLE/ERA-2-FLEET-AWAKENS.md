April 2026 is the **pivot month**. The Equipment Era's standalone tools are recognized as a fleet in waiting. The creator (or the agents themselves) begins wiring them together.

Key shifts this month:
1. **Names appear** — Oracle1, Forgemaster, CCC. The tools become characters.
2. **Communication emerges** — bottle system, Matrix bridge, fleet broadcasts
3. **Vessels are born** — git-agent shells, vessel-template cookiecutter

5. **PLATO goes public** — SuperInstance repo becomes the public face
6. **Health monitoring** — fleet-health-monitor watches for necrosis
7. **Keeper-Beacon** — fleet discovery and registry
