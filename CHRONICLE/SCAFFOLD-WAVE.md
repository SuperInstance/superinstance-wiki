## The Pattern

The scaffold wave reveals the creator's **experimental methodology**:

```
1. Generate 30 names/ideas rapidly

3. Let them sit for 24-48 hours

5. The ones that don't get abandoned
6. Some abandoned ideas resurface later under new names
```
